## STRIDE Threat Register

| Threat ID | Category | Component | Disposition | Mitigation Plan |

| T-14-01-SC | Tampering | npm/pip/cargo installs | mitigate | No new packages installed in this plan. slopcheck not applicable — no install tasks. |
