<task type="checkpoint:human-verify" gate="blocking">
  <what-built>
    All four typography requirements implemented across Plans 01–03:
    - TYPO-01: Plus Jakarta Sans added to fonts and set as default
    - TYPO-02: Font size sliders extended to 200px maximum in Subtitles and Titles tabs
    - TYPO-03: Bold/Italic toggle rows in Subtitles tab and Titles tab form
    - TYPO-04: Outer Glow section card with color, intensity, and softness controls
  </what-built>
  <how-to-verify>
    Open the studio in your browser: http://localhost:3123

    Subtitles tab verification:
    1. TYPO-01: Open the font family dropdown — "PlusJakartaSans" should appear at the top.
       Select it. Confirm the subtitle text in the preview changes to Plus Jakarta Sans.
    2. TYPO-02: Drag the Font Size slider past 120 toward 200. Confirm the preview subtitle text
       grows larger beyond the old 120px limit. Value should display up to 200.
    3. TYPO-03 (Bold): The "Font Weight" row should show "Regular" and "Bold" buttons. "Bold"
       is selected by default. Click "Regular" — confirm the subtitle text becomes visibly thinner.
       Click "Bold" — confirm it returns to bold weight.
    4. TYPO-03 (Italic): The "Font Style" row should show "Normal" and "Italic" buttons.
       Click "Italic" — confirm the subtitle text slants. Click "Normal" — confirm it returns upright.
    5. TYPO-04: Scroll to the "Outer Glow" section card. Check the checkbox to enable it.
       A color picker, intensity slider, and softness slider should appear.
       Set Softness to 20 and Intensity to 0.8. Confirm a visible soft white halo appears around
       the subtitle text in the preview.

    Titles tab verification (click the Titles tab):
    6. Click "Add Title", fill in text, and look at the form fields.
       Confirm Font Weight and Font Style toggle rows appear after the font family selects.
       Confirm the Outer Glow card appears after the toggles.
       Confirm the title font size input accepts values up to 200.

    Report: describe what you see for each item above. Note any missing controls, wrong placement,
    visual rendering issues, or TypeScript errors in the browser console.
  </how-to-verify>
  <resume-signal>Type "approved" if all 6 items verified, or describe which items failed/look wrong</resume-signal>
