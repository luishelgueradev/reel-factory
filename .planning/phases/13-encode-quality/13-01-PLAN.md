<objective>
Switch `silence-cutter._concatenate_segments` from a full H.264 + AAC re-encode to stream-copy (`-c copy`), eliminating one entire lossy generation pass between the cut/concat step and the finalizer. Add a `validate_concat_mode` validator that inspects the concat output via ffprobe and asserts no fresh libx264 encoder tag was written — making the stream-copy invariant test-enforceable.

Purpose: Closes ENC-01 (no redundant re-encode) and is the upstream half of ENC-05 (duration parity / A-V sync preserved). The finalizer becomes the only pre-Remotion lossy encode after this change.

Output: Modified `cut_video.py` + extended `validate.py` in the silence-cutter service. No new modules, no new env vars, no Docker Compose changes.
</objective>
